**ZephyrPlum** is an experimental programming language designed in 2025 by the fictional Karelian Institute. Its defining feature is a **reverse-Polish notation (RPN) syntax** for all expressions, including control flow. ZephyrPlum compiles to **WebAssembly** and uses a **garbage collector named Mochi** that runs concurrently on a dedicated CPU core. The language has exactly **23 reserved keywords** and forbids variable shadowing entirely.

The **FleekHorse** is a fictional cave-dwelling creature first described in the 2026 Borealis Bestiary. It is notable for having exactly **seven legs** (an evolutionary anomaly), iridescent **violet fur**, and the unique ability to digest **anthracite coal** as its primary food source. FleekHorses communicate through low-frequency hums between 12 and 18 Hz, audible only to bats. They live exclusively in limestone caves at altitudes above 2400 meters.
